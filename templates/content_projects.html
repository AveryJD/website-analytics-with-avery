NHL Player Cards

I've created NHL player stat cards that combine advanced analytics, custom models, and clean visuals to highlight player performance. You can view over 13,000 individual cards of forwards, defenseman, and goalies from 17 seasons! This is still a work in progress, so the rankings and visuals are constantly evolving as I continue improving these cards.

If you have any feedback or find any bugs/errors, I'd love to hear about it (check out my socials to easily contact me). You can also explore the code and the behind the scenes of this project and follow its development on my GitHub.
