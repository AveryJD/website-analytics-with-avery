Player Card Explanation

All attributes are weighted based on the past 3 seasons, with the most recent season weighted the most. This gives a better overview of a player's overall skill instead of only single-season performance.

Each ranking section has the player's ranking for that attribute (the large number on the left), out of the total number of players that qualify for that attribute (the small number on the bottom), and a percentile bar showing the percentage of players that the selected player ranks above in that attribute (the bar on the right).

Graphs show the yearly attribute percentiles, not the weighted percentiles. This gives a clearer view of how player performance changes from season to season.

To qualify for rankings, skaters must have played at least 3.66 minutes per the total games in the season (300 minutes over an 82 game season), goalies must have played 15% of the total games in the season (13 games in an 82 game season).

Data includes all games played up to and including April 17, 2026.

Data used is from Natural Stat Trick.

NHL team logos and player images are the property of the NHL and its teams.

Profile Information

  • Position - The player's position (forward, defense, or goalie).
  • Role - The player's role on his team based on time on ice for skaters and games played for goalies.
  • Age - The player's age as of September 1 of the first year of the selected season.
  • Height - The player's height (in feet and inches).
  • Weight - The player's weight (in pounds).
  • Drafted - The player's overall draft position, the round they were drafted in, and the year they were drafted.
  • Country - The player's country of origin.

Skater Stats

  • Games - The skater's number of games played.
  • Goals - The skater's number of goals in all situations.
  • Assists - The skater's number of assists in all situations.
  • Points - The skater's number of points (goals and assists) in all situations.
  • xGoals - The skater's expected goals in all situations.
  • 5v5 GF % - The skater's 5-on-5 goals share (the percentage of goals the player's team scores versus concedes while the player is on the ice).
  • 5v5 xGF % - The skater's 5-on-5 expected goals share (the percentage of expected goals the player's team scores versus concedes while the player is on the ice).

Skater Rankings

  • 5v5 Offense - The skater's offensive performance at 5-on-5 (even strength), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • 5v5 Defense - The skater's defensive performance at 5-on-5 (even strength), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • 5v4 Offense - The skater's offensive performance at 5-on-4 (the power play), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate. Requires averaging over 45 seconds of 5v4 ice time per game.
  • 4v5 Defense - The skater's defensive performance at 4-on-5 (the penalty kill), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate. Requires averaging over 45 seconds of 4v5 ice time per game.
  • On-Ice Offense - The offensive performance of the skater's team at 5-on-5 while the skater is on the ice, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • On-Ice Defense - The defensive performance of the skater's team at 5-on-5 while the skater is on the ice, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Shooting - The skater's shooting performance at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Scoring - The skater's scoring performance at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Playmaking - The skater's playmaking performance at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Penalties - The skater's performance at drawing penalties versus take penalties at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Physicality - The skater's hit-delevering performance at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Faceoffs - The skater's faceoff performance at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate. Requires averaging at least 3 faceoffs per game.
  • O-Zone Starts - The frequency of a skater's shifts starting in the offensive zone at 5-on-5, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Fantasy - The skater's fantasy points production in a typical fantasy league, adjusted to a per game rate.

Skater Graph

  • Red Line - The skater's yearly 5v5 offense percentile over the past 5 seasons.
  • Blue Line - The skater's yearly 5v5 defense percentile over the past 5 seasons.
  • Dashed Light Red Line - The skater's yearly 5v4 offense percentile over the past 5 seasons.
  • Dashed Light Blue Line - The skater's yearly 4v5 defense percentile over the past 5 seasons.

Goalie Stats

  • Games - The goalie's number of games played.
  • Record - The goalie's record (wins-losses-overtime/shootout losses).
  • Shutouts - The goalie's number of shutouts.
  • Save % - The goalie's save percentage in all situations.
  • GAA - The goalie's goals against average.
  • GSAx - The goalie's goals saved above expected.
  • GSAx/60 - The goalie's goals saved above expected, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.

Goalie Rankings

  • Overall - The goalie's performance across all situations, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Even Strength - The goalie's performance at 5-on-5 (even strength), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Penalty Kill - The goalie's performance on the penalty kill (4-on-5), adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Low Danger - The goalie's performance at stopping low-danger shots, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Medium Danger - The goalie's performance at stopping medium-danger shots, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • High Danger - The goalie's performance at stopping high-danger shots, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Rebounds - The goalie's performance at limiting rebound attempts, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Team Defense - Reflects the quality and quantity of shots a goalie faces. Higher rankings indicate stronger team defense, adjusted to a per 60 minute rate.
  • Great Starts - The goalie's frequency of starts with a save percentage ≥0.915, adjusted to a per game rate.
  • Quality Starts - The goalie's frequency of starts with a save percentage ≥0.900, adjusted to a per game rate.
  • Bad Starts - The goalie's frequency of starts with a save percentage <0.900, adjusted to a per game rate.
  • Awful Starts - The goalie's frequency of starts with a save percentage ≤0.885, adjusted to a per game rate.
  • Fantasy - The goalie's fantasy points production in a typical fantasy league, adjusted to a per game rate.

Goalie Graph

  • Dashed Light Red Line - The goalie's yearly even-strength (5v5) percentile over the past 5 seasons.
  • Dashed Light Blue Line - The goalie's yearly penalty kill (4v5) percentile over the past 5 seasons.
