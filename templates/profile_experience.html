Other Experience

Lifeguard - Municipality of the District of Guysborough

I worked as a pool lifeguard each summer during June-August from 2021 to 2024 (12 months). In my third summer, I became a national lifeguard and swim instructor, earning my National Lifeguard and Swim Instructor certifications. This job strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and decision-making skills while emphasizing responsibility and clear communication.
